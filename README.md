# Dino Toskic | PhD Candidate in Economics

I am a PhD Candidate in Economics specializing in Applied Microeconomic, and Public Policy Evaluation. My research focuses on the intersection of firm-level dynamics and government intervention, with a particular emphasis on the Italian productivity landscape.

## 🔬 Research Interests
* **Public Policy Evaluation:** Impact of state aid and subsidies on firm performance.
* **Firm Dynamics:** Analysis of productivity, capital structure, and survival in the SME sector.
* **Big Data in Economics:** Utilizing large-scale administrative and commercial datasets (AIDA, RNA).

## 📊 Working Papers & Projects
### "State Aid and Corporate Performance: Evidence from 700k Italian Firms"
*Developing an identification strategy to estimate the causal effect of public incentives on EBITDA and investment cycles.*
* **Data:** AIDA (Bureau van Dijk) – Full panel 2014-2023.
* **Methodology:** High-dimensional Fixed Effects (HDFE), Staggered Difference-in-Differences.
